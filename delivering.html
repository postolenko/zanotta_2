How Use Cookies and what are they?

Cookies are small text files that are stored within your internet browser by the websites that you visit. Like many other websites, LuxDeco.com places cookies on your device to understand more about your visit to help us to enhance your shopping experience at LuxDeco.com.


Cookies are used for various aspects of functionality such as keeping track of what items are in your shopping bag, saving your preferences and learning more about how you arrived at our website.


Cookie Types:

1. Essential Cookies

LuxDeco.com needs essential cookies in order to function and if they are not accepted by you, parts of the LuxDeco.com won’t perform as they should. Examples of where essential cookies are used include: storing how many items are in your basket, to identify when you are signed in, and to determine which currency we should use based on your preferred delivery location when displaying prices.


2. Third Party Cookies

We allow carefully-selected third parties to set cookies during your visit to LuxDeco.com. These organisations provide us with information on how you use the site. They may also use cookies to promote our newest products and latest offers to you on other websites based on your activity at LuxDeco.com. For example, you may see products that you viewed at LuxDeco.com presented on other websites as you move around the internet. Some of our partners will use your location to provide more relevant messaging. If you wish to switch this feature off continue reading.
